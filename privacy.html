Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Velocity Auto Parts. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our products.

Information We Collect

To provide our services effectively and improve your shopping experience, we collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details such as email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment information you provide during account creation or checkout.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device, IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, and other diagnostic data collected automatically when you use our website.
  • Usage Data: Details of your visits to our website including pages viewed, how long you spend on specific pages, and navigation paths.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Process your orders swiftly and accurately, ensuring proper delivery and billing.
  • Communicate with you about your account, orders, updates, and promotions (only if you opt in).
  • Improve our website functionality, personalize your experience, and prevent fraud or unauthorized activity.
  • Analyze data trends to enhance product selection and service quality.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party companies who assist us in delivering services, including:

  • Payment processors and financial institutions to complete transactions.
  • Shipment and fulfillment partners to ensure timely delivery of orders.
  • Marketing and analytics providers, strictly governed by contractual privacy obligations.

We ensure all third-party partners adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

Your Rights and Choices

You have rights regarding your personal data, such as:

  • Accessing and receiving a copy of your data.
  • Correcting or updating inaccurate data.
  • Requesting deletion of data where applicable.
  • Opting out of marketing communications at any time.

Please contact us if you wish to exercise any of these rights and we will respond promptly.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

For details about how we use cookies and similar tracking tools, please visit our Cookies Policy. These technologies improve your browsing experience and help us understand how our site is used.

Security of Your Information

We use technical and organizational measures such as encryption, restricted access, and monitoring to safeguard your personal information against misuse, loss, or unauthorized access.

Data Retention

We keep your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and comply with legal obligations. Data no longer needed is securely deleted or anonymized.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If a parent or guardian discovers that their child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately for removal.

International Users

If you access our website from outside our home country, your data may be transferred to and processed in our country. We take steps to ensure your privacy rights are respected no matter where you are located.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updated versions will be posted on this page with the effective date noted at the top.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@velocityautoparts.com.
