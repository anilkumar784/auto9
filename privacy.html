Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Velocity Auto Parts. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our products.

Information We Collect: We collect personal information such as your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment details to process orders and provide customer support. We also collect non-personal data such as browsing habits and device information using cookies to enhance your user experience.

Use of Information: The information you provide helps us process transactions, communicate order status, improve our products and services, and send promotional offers if you opt-in. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Protection: We implement strict security measures including SSL encryption and firewalls to keep your information safe from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our employees are trained on data privacy best practices and access your data only when necessary.

Third-Party Services: We may share your information with trusted service providers such as payment processors, shipping companies, and analytics services required to fulfill our business operations. All partners are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Your Rights: You have the right to review, correct, or delete your personal information stored with us. You may unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting our support team.

Children’s Privacy: Our website is not directed toward children under 13 years old and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Policy Updates: We may update our privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in legal requirements or our business practices. Updated versions will be posted on this page with the revision date.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact us at privacy@velocityautoparts.com.
