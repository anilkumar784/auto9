Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Velocity Auto Parts (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to improve your experience and provide you with personalized services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites recognize your device and remember information about your preferences or actions. Cookies make browsing smoother and more tailored to your interests and needs.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and shopping cart functionality. Without these, some services may not be available.
  • Performance Cookies: We use these cookies to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website. This data helps us understand which pages are most popular, and how users interact with the site, so we can improve its performance and usability.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices, such as language settings or region, enabling a more personalized browsing experience. They also allow us to remember login credentials if you create an account on our site.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies help us deliver ads that are relevant to your interests by tracking your browsing activity on our website and third-party sites. We do not share any personally identifiable information with advertisers.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies enable us to enhance your visit to Velocity Auto Parts by remembering choices you’ve made, understanding your interests, and ensuring you have a smooth and efficient shopping experience. For example, cookies help us keep track of products you add to your cart and recognize returning visitors for a faster login or personalized offers.

Third-Party Cookies

We may permit trusted third-party partners such as analytics providers, advertising networks, and social media platforms to set cookies on our website. These third parties use cookies to analyze browsing patterns and display targeted advertisements on other websites or social networks. For instance, Google Analytics helps us understand visitor behavior to improve our site, while social media plugins might use cookies to enhance sharing capabilities.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Most web browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies via their settings. You can set your browser to notify you whenever a cookie is being placed, giving you the opportunity to accept or decline cookies on an individual basis.

Disabling cookies, especially essential or functional ones, may impact your ability to use certain features of the website, such as shopping cart functionality or personalized settings. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled to enjoy the full benefits of our services.

Cookie Consent

Upon visiting our website, you may be prompted to accept our use of cookies. Your continued use of our site indicates your consent to our cookies as described in this policy. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser preferences.

More Information

For more detailed information about cookies, their purposes, and how to manage them, you may visit All About Cookies, a reliable resource for cookie-related information.
