Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Velocity Auto Parts (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to improve your experience and provide you with personalized services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites recognize your device and remember information about your preferences or actions.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for basic website functionality, such as secure login and shopping cart management.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous data about website usage to help us improve site performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences, such as language and region, to provide a personalized experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: These may be used to deliver relevant ads based on your browsing activity, but we do not share personal data with advertisers.

Managing Cookies: Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can change your settings to decline or delete cookies if you prefer. Disabling some cookies might limit your access to certain parts of our site or impact website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies: We may allow third-party service providers such as analytics and advertising partners to place cookies when you visit our site. These third parties are governed by their own privacy policies.

Updates to This Policy: We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with the effective date.

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@velocityautoparts.com.
